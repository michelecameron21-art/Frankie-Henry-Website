1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
============================================================

Frankie & Henry sits at a unique intersection of several niches:
- Yorkshire Terrier / Yorkie children's books (VERY low competition)
- African adventure children's books (moderate competition)
- Courage/bravery/friendship themes (high competition but lots of long-tail opportunities)
- Picture books for ages 4-8 / read-aloud books (massive search volume)

The BIGGEST opportunity: There are almost NO children's picture books
featuring a Yorkshire Terrier on an African adventure. This ultra-specific
niche means you can own it with the right SEO strategy.

6. NICHE GAP ANALYSIS
============================================================

GAPS WHERE FRANKIE & HENRY CAN DOMINATE:

1. "Yorkshire Terrier children's picture book adventure"
   Competition: Almost ZERO. Only a handful of Yorkie kids' books
   exist (Dorky Yorkie, Yippy Yappy Yorkie, Yorkie Tales). NONE
   combine Yorkies with African adventure. This is YOUR niche.

2. "Small dog African adventure children's book"
   Competition: ZERO found. No books combine a small breed dog
   with an African wilderness adventure.

3. "River rescue children's picture book"
   Competition: Very low. Most "rescue" books are about rescuing
   shelter dogs, not dramatic river rescue adventures.

4. "Children's book Yorkie and hippo" / "Yorkie and crocodile"
   Competition: ZERO. Unique character pairings that nobody owns.

5. "Brave little dog saves the day picture book"
   Competition: Low. Some brave dog books exist, but few with
   the specific African wilderness + river rescue angle.

CONTENT GAPS (Topics with search demand but few quality results):

- Behind-the-scenes of creating a children's picture book
- "What Yorkshire Terriers are really like" (for kids)
- African wildlife facts for kids (tied to your story)
- Free printable activities based on African adventure themes
- Read-aloud tips for parents (tied to your book)

7. COMPETITION ASSESSMENT
============================================================

"Yorkshire Terrier children's book":
- Competition level: VERY LOW
- Existing competitors: Yorkie Tales (yorkietales.com), Dorky Yorkie,
  The Yippy Yappy Yorkie (Debbie Macomber)
- Assessment: Very few illustrated adventure stories. Most are either
  breed education books or simple character stories. None feature
  African adventures. Easy to differentiate.

"African adventure children's book":
- Competition level: MODERATE
- Key competitors: "An African Adventure" by CB Crew (Amazon),
  curated lists from bookgirlsguide.com, Brightly, BU African
  Studies Center
- Assessment: Several books exist but most are educational or
  cultural. Few are ACTION adventure stories. The dog + Africa
  combination is unique.

"Children's book about courage / friendship":
- Competition level: HIGH
- Key competitors: The Lion Inside (Rachel Bright), Brave (Stacy
  McAnulty), numerous titles on every major book list
- Assessment: Extremely crowded category. But combining courage
  with the specific Yorkie + Africa angle creates a unique niche.
  Don't try to rank for "courage" alone -- always combine with
  your unique elements.

"Read aloud books for 4-8 year olds":
- Competition level: VERY HIGH
- Key competitors: Every major children's book, Mo Willems,
  Magic Tree House, massive recommendation lists
- Assessment: Cannot compete head-on. But you CAN create content
  like "best read-aloud adventure books about animals" or
  "read-aloud books about dogs for kids" where competition drops.

8. CONTENT STRATEGY RECOMMENDATIONS
============================================================

A. ESSENTIAL WEBSITE PAGES (SEO-optimized):

1. BOOK PAGE: "Frankie & Henry: The Brave River Rescue"
   - Full synopsis optimized for keywords
   - Title tag: "Frankie & Henry: The Brave River Rescue | A Yorkshire
     Terrier African Adventure Picture Book for Ages 4-8"
   - Include: reviews, awards, buy links, read-aloud video
   - Alt text on all images with keywords

2. ABOUT THE AUTHORS page
   - Establishes E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust)
   - Include why you wrote the book, your connection to the themes

3. ABOUT THE CHARACTERS page
   - "Meet Frankie" and "Meet Henry" sections
   - Optimized for "Yorkie children's book character" searches
   - Fun facts about Yorkshire Terriers for kids

B. BLOG CONTENT TO CREATE (in priority order):

1. "Why Yorkshire Terriers Make the Best Adventure Heroes"
   - Targets: Yorkie picture book, brave little dog, terrier adventure
   - Links to your book page

2. "10 Amazing African Animals Kids Will Love Learning About"
   - Targets: African animals for kids, African wildlife children's book
   - Include animals from your story (hippos, crocodiles, etc.)

3. "Teaching Kids About Courage: A Parent's Guide with Book Ideas"
   - Targets: children's book about courage, picture book bravery
   - Feature YOUR book alongside well-known titles

4. "The Best Adventure Picture Books to Read Aloud"
   - Targets: read aloud adventure books, picture books for kids
   - Curated list format (these DOMINATE page 1 results)
   - Include your book naturally among other picks

5. "Free Printable Activities: African Adventure for Kids"
   - Targets: African adventure printable, children's book activities
   - Coloring pages, word searches, maze featuring F&H characters
   - Massive link-building and sharing potential

6. "Behind the Scenes: How We Created Frankie & Henry"
   - Targets: making a children's picture book, illustrated book process
   - Shows illustrations, drafts, character development
   - Builds E-E-A-T authority

C. TECHNICAL SEO CHECKLIST:

- Every page needs a unique title tag with primary keyword
- Meta descriptions for all pages (150-160 characters, include keywords)
- All images need descriptive alt text (e.g., "Frankie the Yorkshire
  Terrier crossing a river in Africa - illustrated children's book")
- Schema markup: Book schema on the book page, Organization schema
  on the home page, Article schema on blog posts
- Fast page load speed (under 3 seconds)
- Mobile-friendly design
- Internal links between all pages
- XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
