Welcome to the Frankie & Henry website, operated by Liabri Studios. We take your privacy seriously — especially because our books are made for children. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Who We Are

Liabri Studios is the publisher of the Frankie & Henry children's book series. This website is operated by Michele Cameron. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at:
Email: frankiehenryadventures@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

We only collect information that you choose to give us. This includes:

  • Email address — if you sign up for our newsletter via the "Join the Pack" form on our website.

We do not collect names, addresses, payment details, or any other personal information through this website.

3. Children's Privacy

The Frankie & Henry books are aimed at children ages 4–8, but this website and our newsletter are intended for parents, carers, and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

4. How We Use Your Information

If you sign up for our newsletter, we will use your email address to send you:

  • News about new Frankie & Henry books and upcoming releases
  • Exclusive free printables, activity sheets, and resources
  • Behind-the-scenes updates and Wild Place news

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Email Marketing

Our newsletter is managed through MailerLite, a third-party email platform. By signing up, your email address is stored securely on MailerLite's servers. You can view MailerLite's privacy policy at mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy.

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send. We will never send you emails after you have unsubscribed.

6. Cookies

When you visit our website, a cookie banner will ask for your consent. We use two types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the website to function (e.g. remembering your cookie preference). These are always active.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. These are only set if you click "Accept all".

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can change your cookie preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

7. How We Store Your Data

Your email address is stored securely with MailerLite and is only accessible to Liabri Studios. We retain your data for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. When you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our active mailing list.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time (by unsubscribing)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at frankiehenryadventures@gmail.com.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites such as Amazon, Instagram, and TikTok. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to read their individual privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to check back occasionally.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please get in touch:
